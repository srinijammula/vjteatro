ABHAY

This story revolves around a boy who is from an economically poor and lower caste family. This boy faces lot of problems from his earlier stages of life getting ragged or bullied by people on the basis of his caste and appearance due to which he slowly gets down into depression and tries to end life. At the same time,the only person who supported his entire life journey was his MOTHER. She is the only one who kept strengthening him , whenever he sounded depressed. This boy always wanted people around him but he never had one. His mother always guided him saying that never ever give up in any situation. And gave him positive vibes declaring that even he was good enough.
